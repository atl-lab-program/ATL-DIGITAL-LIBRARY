Percy Jackson 1

Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief is the first book in the hit fantasy series by
Rick Riordan. It follows a troubled, dyslexic boy who discovers he is actually a demigod—the
son of the Greek god Poseidon. He leaves his difficult mortal life for a secret training camp,
where he becomes best friends with Annabeth Chase and Grover Underwood, and together, they
embark on a dangerous quest across America to find Zeus's stolen master bolt and prevent an
apocalyptic war among the gods.
